JIAXIANG LIU

👨‍💻 Profile

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Guangdong Institute of Intelligence Science and Technology (GDIIST), working with Prof. Luping Shi (Tsinghua University). I received my Ph.D. in December 2025 from Zhejiang University, under the supervision of Prof. Zuozhu Liu. Before that, I was a Research Intern at A*STAR Singapore (Sep 2023–Sep 2024) with Prof. Joey Tianyi Zhou, and obtained my Bachelor's degree from Jiangxi Normal University in 2018.
My research interests lie in trustworthy multimodal AI for medicine, with a focus on medical vision–language reasoning, chain-of-thought for VQA, adversarial robustness of CLIP, and emerging work on brain decoding.

I have authored 32 peer-reviewed papers (9 first-author, 8 co-first, 3 corresponding, 12 co-author), including Cell Press Patterns, Nature Communications (IF 15.7), ICML, KDD, NeurIPS, AAAI, EMNLP, MICCAI, ACL, and IEEE TCSVT (IF 11.1) / TMM / TIM / TETCI. H-index 15, i10-index 22.
