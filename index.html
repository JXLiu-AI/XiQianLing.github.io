hello, i'm jiaxiang.
I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Guangdong Institute of Intelligence Science and Technology (GDIIST), advised by Prof. Luping Shi (Tsinghua University). I received my Ph.D. from Zhejiang University in 2025 under Prof. Zuozhu Liu, and was previously a Research Intern at A*STAR Singapore with Prof. Joey Tianyi Zhou.

My research focuses on trustworthy multimodal AI for medicine, building reliable medical vision-language models via multi-expert chain-of-thought reasoning and adversarial robustness, extending to brain decoding.

I have authored 32 peer-reviewed papers (9 independent first-author, 8 co-first, 3 corresponding, 12 co-author), including Cell Press Patterns, Nature Communications (IF 15.7), ICML, KDD, NeurIPS, AAAI, EMNLP, MICCAI, ACL, and IEEE TCSVT (IF 11.1) / TMM / TIM / TETCI. H-index 15, i10-index 22 (Google Scholar).

I serve as a reviewer for ICML (silver tier, 2026), NeurIPS, ICLR, KDD, CVPR, ECCV, MICCAI, AAAI, IEEE TCSVT, and IEEE TIM.
